Microsoft Bookshelf provides fast, easy access to 6 popular reference works to give you the resources you need to create superb documents, reports, and presentations. Use it to enhance your work with comprehensive, up-to-date information, or extend your search to the Internet with over 5,000 editorially selected links. With Microsoft Bookshelf - British Reference Collection you get :

Fast Access: The QuickShelf shortcut makes it easy to search from any Windows based application.
Comprehensive Resources: Bookshelf® includes 6 frequently used reference titles including
  • The Chambers Dictionary (500,000 definitions)
  • The Hutchinson Concise Encyclopedia (20,000 entries)
  • Roget Longman's Thesaurus (250,000 synonyms and antonyms)
  • Bloomsbury Treasury of Quotations (20,000 quotations)
  • Microsoft Bookshelf Internet Directory (5,000 informative sites)
  • Concise Encarta World Atlas (70 political and topographic maps)


Powerful results: Enhance your projects with quotations, synonyms, pictures, maps and more from the Bookshelf reference sources.
