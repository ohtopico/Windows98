Microsoft® Money 98 Financial Suite

Money 98 Financial Suite is an all-in-one solution, delivering complete capabilities to help you take charge of the full spectrum of your financial activities – from daily transactions to long-term planning and investing.

Experience the first product on the market to advance the Personal Finance Management (PFM) category from its origins as an "electronic chequebook" to a true financial assistant. As you work throughout the product, you'll continuously benefit from personalised guidance and easy access to the most current financial information through its enhanced Internet integration.

  • Expert Assistant - Receive intelligent, personalised advice for making sound financial decisions. The Expert Assistant will help you prioritize transactions, follow your budget, simplify complex decisions, avoid financial mistakes, and recognise financial opportunities while you work throughout the product.
  • Goal Planner - Quickly set up, track and achieve your short and long-term financial goals, then have Money monitor your progress, while you refine your plan using features like the new Debt Reduction Planner.
  • Tax Estimator - Money’s Tax Estimator has been developed to help you estimate your tax situation and help you to prepare for submitting Inland Revenue Tax Returns. This feature is particularly beneficial for all users affected by Inland Revenue Self-Assessment. The Tax Estimator also works with leading tax preparation products such as Which? TaxCalc or SmartTax
  • Microsoft Money Manager - Make informed decisions by browsing current articles and data on a variety of financial topics. The CD-ROM includes many articles previously published by the Consumer Association through the Which? magazine and all other articles have been produced for Money by leading UK financial experts such as Janet Walford, Editor Money Management, Nils Blyth, Television and Radio financial presentor – plus you'll receive current information each time you go online. Use helpful worksheets to help you compare loans, track your home inventory, determine how much house you can afford.
  • Monthly Report - Receive an automatically generated report which provides a comprehensive overview of your finances, a summary of the month's activities throughout the program, status on your goal plan, and a preview of the month ahead – we'll even notify you when it's ready!
