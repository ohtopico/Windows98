Microsoft® AutoRoute Express Europe 6.0
The Complete Easy-To-Use Travel Planner


Microsoft AutoRoute Express Europe 98 (TM) is an exceptional, easy-to-use CD-ROM based travel product that makes planning a trip as much fun as the journey itself. With the most comprehensive mapping data available, including more than 1.72 million miles / 2.7 million kilometers of roads, and a powerful new routing engine that calculates even long complex trips in under a minute, AutoRoute Express Europe 98 takes vacation and business travel planning to the next level.
