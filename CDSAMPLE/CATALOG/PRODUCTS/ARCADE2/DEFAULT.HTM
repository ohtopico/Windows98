Microsoft® Return of Arcade
Relive those carefree and exciting days when every quarter burned a hole in your pocket.

Microsoft Return of Arcade puts you in command of four hot arcade classics—Pac-Man™, Pole Position, Dig-Dug™ and Galaxian™—replicated exactly for the PC.
