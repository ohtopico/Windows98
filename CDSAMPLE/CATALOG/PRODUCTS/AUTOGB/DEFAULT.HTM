Microsoft® AutoRoute Express Great Britain 6.0
The Complete Easy-To-Use Travel Planner for Great Britain.

Microsoft AutoRoute Express Great Britain 98 (TM) is an exceptional, easy-to-use CD-ROM travel product that makes planning a trip as much fun as the journey itself.

  • Use the Route Wizard to plan a trip quickly from more than 143,000 miles of roads in England, Scotland, and Wales from the latest 1:250,000 Ordnance Survey maps.
  • With the 98 edition, you'll get two map styles to choose from roadway and terrain, plus a comprehensive database of thousands of articles and photographs from our dedicated editorial team of European travel experts.
  • There's over 4,200 sights and attractions listed, plus information on 850 golf courses, 130 football clubs, 75 ski resorts, and 240 museums.
  • Access more than 900 listings from the Les Routiers hotel and restaurant guide and over 250 campsite reviews from the Alan Rogers' Good Camps guide.
  • Great new multimedia content, 450 scenic photographs, panoramic views, and updated Internet links make the best trip planning information just a mouse click away.
