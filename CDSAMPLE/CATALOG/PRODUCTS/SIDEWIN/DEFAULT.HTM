Microsoft® SideWinder™
Play with Smooth, Comfortable Control

Microsoft SideWinder is a high-value joystick, bringing new levels of quality and control to the generalist game player. Its stylish lines, comfortably moulded handle, solid durability, and smooth control place it a cut-above the competition. SideWinder includes a primary and secondary fire button, along with a throttle for controlling speed in the latest games. It is suitable for anyone demanding a high-quality game control device for a reasonable price.

Recommended for Ages: 10 and up.
Microsoft SideWinder Homepage
  • Buttons The two buttons on the handle, button 1 and button 2, correspond to conventional joystick buttons. Button 1 is the trigger. Button 2 may be a secondary "fire" button, depending on your game. Button 1 and button 2 are often used to fire and select weapons, respectively.

  • Throttle Control The throttle control on the base gives you the power to adjust incremental game functions. Uses of the throttle control vary, depending on your game. The throttle control is often used to adjust thrust and throttle and change altitude.

  • Trim Controls Use the trim controls to adjust your joystick's movement on the x and y axes, to reduce drift, and to return your joystick to a neutral position.
