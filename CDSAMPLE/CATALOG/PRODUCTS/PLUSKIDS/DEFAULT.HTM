Microsoft® Plus! for Kids
Make Windows® 95 Even More Secure and Fun for Kids!

With Microsoft Plus! For Kids, you can have quick, easy control over your home computer’s use. Parental controls allow you to set up profiles for each family member. With engaging creativity tools to inspire them, kids can explore endlessly within their own personalised desktop.


Recommended for kids ages 3-12.
Microsoft Kids Homepage

Video
  • Kids can personalise their experience with any one of 10 fun desktop themes, each with animated icons, backgrounds, and screen savers.
  • Spark your kids’ imaginations. Three full-featured creativity programs, plus clip art and fonts, encourage them to create and play.
  • Talk It! Type in text and listen to your words in one of 20 funny and surprising voices.
  • Play It! Tap out a rhythm, record a song, play on the keyboard, and sing along!
  • Paint It! Create paintings using amazing paintbrushes, patterns, and effects.
  • Picture Picker Choose from more than 1,000 cool clip-art images for paintings
  • Protect It! limits access to your personal files and programs and lets you determine Internet ratings for your PC, so only appropriate sites are accessible.
