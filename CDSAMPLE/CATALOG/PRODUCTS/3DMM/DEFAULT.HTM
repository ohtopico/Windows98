Microsoft® 3D Movie Maker
Turn Your Ideas Into Awesome 3D Movies!

You're the Director! Create cool 3D movies with amazing sets, actors, special effects, and music! Whatever you want to do, McZee® is always nearby to lend a hand.


Recommended for kids 8 and older
